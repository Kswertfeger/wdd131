Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to share different PC components, with people who are seeking information on pc parts. You will be able to search for different pc parts on the child page based on the brand, and compenent.

Audience

The audience for this site are people who are interested in computers or building them.

Dynamic elements

Random images of computers will be selected every 30 seconds or so on the site. Child page will have a search feature to search for PC Parts.
